A little about us...

A small business for a small business.

Nice Lion Technologies is a small web and software development company based in Clemson, South Carolina. We partner with small businesses to help give them a strong presence on the web to drive sales and retain customers.

Though Nice Lion Technologies did not legally organize until 2021, the idea started way back in 2014 with the launch of the iOS app, OrangeBlock. Since then, Nice Lion Technologies has published various applications, open source projects, and hardware projects under the pseudonym "Nice Lion."

On July 7th, 2021, Nice Lion Technologies LLC legally organized in the State of South Carolina.
